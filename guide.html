Local Guide
Beaches

Blackpool Sands

1.1 miles

Award winning blue-flag beach with great facilities and paddle board rental. 15-20 minutes walk from the house (up hill on the way back). The beach is sand/shingle and can shelve steeply at times.

Strete Gate

3.7 miles

North end of Slapton Sands, a pebble beach which stretches for almost two miles from Strete Gate to the village of Torcross. Wild bird reserve and freshwater lake behind the beaches. 10 minutes in the car or just over an hours walk (steep in places).

Torcross

5.8 miles

South end of Slapton Sands, a pebble beach which stretches for almost two miles from Strete Gate to the village of Torcross. Wild bird reserve and freshwater lake behind the beaches. 15 minutes in the car.

Gara Rock

13.7 miles

Sand beach surrounded by cliffs. Car park next to the Gara Rock Hotel and restaurant. Fairly steep 10 minute walk down to the beach.

East Portlemouth

14.3 miles

East Portlemouth and Mill Bay are on the opposite side of the estuary to Salcombe. They are both beautiful sandy beaches with amazing views over to Salcombe. At low tide you can walk from one to the other, and the beaches can also be reached from Salcombe via a small passenger ferry. Park in the village at East Portlemouth and walk down the fisherman's steps.

Mill Bay

15.5 miles

East Portlemouth and Mill Bay are on the opposite side of the estuary to Salcombe. They are both beautiful sandy beaches with amazing views over to Salcombe. At low tide you can walk from one to the other, and the beaches can also be reached from Salcombe via a small passenger ferry. There's a NT car park at Mill Bay, but we recommend you go early to get a space.

Soar Mill Cove

16.9 miles

Unspoilt beautiful cove best seen at low tide. Parking at the Soar Mill Hotel and walk down to the beach (about 20 mins).

Thurlestone

19.1 miles

Thurlstone and South Milton are next to each other on the coast path, and you can easily walk from one to the other.

South Milton Sands

19.5 miles

Thurlstone and South Milton are next to each other on the coast path, and you can easily walk from one to the other. South Milton is good for rock pooling at low tide and great for sun sets.

Hope Cove

21 miles

Two beaches at either side of this charming seaside village.

Bigbury on Sea

22.5 miles

Striking huge beach at low tide, walk over to Burgh Island with its famous Art Deco hotel. A sea tractor operates when the tide is in.

North Sands

22.8 miles

Large gently shelving sandy beaches, ideal for young children. The beach virtually disappears at high tide. There's a car park at North Sands or free parking on the zig.zag road down to North Sands.

South Sands

23.1 miles

Large gently shelving sandy beaches, ideal for young children. The beach virtually disappears at high tide.

Coleton Fishacre

Kingswear

Beautiful National Trust House and Gardens with access to the coast path. Take either the upper or lower vehicle ferry over the Dart river and follow the signs.

Greenway

Kingswear

The former holiday home of Agatha Christie and her family with amazing views over the Dart River. Parking is very limited and requires booking in advance. Passenger ferrys run regularly from Dartmouth - information can be found on the quayside. It is also possible to walk from Kingswear to Greenway, the views are fabulous but this is quite a strenuous walk, you can return via the Greenway/Dartmouth passenger ferry.

Overbecks Garden

Salcombe

Tropical gardens tucked away on the cliffs above Salcombe with fantastic views. Drive to Salcombe and head towards South Sands.
